Welcome to GoQuiz App

This quiz will contain a total of 10 questions. Each question holds 10 points

Rules are stated below:

  1. A correct question adds 10 points
  2. An incorrect question deducts -5 points
  3. Skipping a question does not add or deduct
  4. Each question has a duration of 40 seconds
  5. A refresh of the page will reset the quiz
  6. A total score of 50 points and above indicates that the quiz was passed
  7. A total score below 50 points indicates that the user failed

Good Luck!
